Day 11: Typography

Topic: CSS for Typography

Theory Content:

    Font properties include font-family, font-size, font-weight, and font-style.
    Text properties include text-align, text-decoration, text-transform, and line-height.
    Web-safe fonts are recommended for cross-browser compatibility.

Notes:

    Setting line-height improves readability.
    Web fonts, like Google Fonts, can be linked externally for more variety.
