Day 13: Flexbox Basics

Topic: Introduction to Flexbox

Theory Content:

    Flexbox is a layout model that allows responsive alignment and spacing of elements.
    Important properties:
        display: flex; to initiate a flex container.
        justify-content for horizontal alignment.
        align-items for vertical alignment.
        flex-direction to control row or column layout.

Notes:

    Flexbox simplifies layout management, especially for centering elements.
    Flex properties adapt well to responsive design.
